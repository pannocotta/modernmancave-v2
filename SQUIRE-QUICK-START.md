## What Squire Does

- **Online booking** - Customers book appointments 24/7 from your website or mobile app
- **Schedule management** - See your day at a glance, manage walk-ins vs appointments
- **Payments** - Process cards, track revenue, automatic invoicing
- **Client database** - Keep notes, track preferences, send reminders
- **Marketing** - Send promos, loyalty programs, automated texts

---

## What It Costs

Pricing varies but typically:
- **~$50-150/month** for the platform (depends on features)
- **2-3% transaction fee** on payments processed through Squire
- **One-time setup** - Usually free with onboarding

**You need to:**
1. Sign up at https://getsquire.com
2. Talk to their sales team to get exact pricing
3. Decide if it's worth it for your volume

## Setup Process (When You're Ready)

### Step 1: You Sign Up
- Go to https://getsquire.com
- Fill out shop details
- Talk to their onboarding team

### Step 2: Squire Configures Your Shop
They'll ask for:
- Business name, address, phone
- Services & pricing (we have this from the /prices page)
- Staff names
- Operating hours
- Bank details for payments

### Step 3: We Connect It to Your Website
Once you have your Squire account:
- You give us your "Shop ID" (they'll provide this)
- We update the booking page (takes 10 minutes)
- We test it
- Goes live

### Step 4: Customers Can Book
- From your website modernmancave.com.au/booking
- From the Squire mobile app (search "Modern Mancave")
- You'll get notifications for each booking

## When to Turn It On

**Good time to activate:**
- When you're consistently busy
- When managing bookings becomes a headache
- When you want to reduce no-shows (upfront payment helps)
- When you have staff who need schedule management

**Not worth it yet if:**
- Still building clientele
- Happy with current manual booking
- Not ready for monthly software costs
- Prefer personal touch of manual scheduling
